Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide resources for parents and children to find books with characters with diversity in nationality and ethnic background. We hope to provide children and adults with the possibility to access books they may not have known about or be able to afford. Learning about a variety of diverse upbringings helps with acceptance and respect among people regardless of their differences. This will hopefully make the world a better place one book at a time.
